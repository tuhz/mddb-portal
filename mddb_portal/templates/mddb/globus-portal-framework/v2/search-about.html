GM/CA @ APS

The General Medical Sciences and Cancer Institutes Structural Biology Facility at the Advanced Photon Source (GM/CA @ APS) is a part of the X-ray Science Divison at APS, Argonne National Laboratory (ANL). The GM/CA @ APS project has been established by the National Institutes of Health's (NIH) National Institute of General Medical Sciences (NIGMS) and National Cancer Institute (NCI) to build and operate a national user facility for crystallographic structure determination of biological macromolecules by X-ray diffraction.
